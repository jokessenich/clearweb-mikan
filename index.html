The Pianist

Fire, Spirit & the Essence of Jazz Piano

Mikan is an American virtuoso jazz pianist with a distinguished career spanning decades. Over the years he has shared the stage with jazz greats such as Tony Scott, Clifford Jordan, Chet Baker, Ernie Wilkins, Ralph Penland, Red Holloway, Charles McPherson, and Louis Hayes, to name but a few.

He has performed and recorded across the US and Europe, bringing his deep passion for jazz to audiences wherever he goes. Mikan presents a broad repertoire drawn from the Great American Songbook, jazz and blues standards, classic ballads, pop, R&B, funk, Bossa Nova, and Samba.

Beyond his life on stage, Mikan is also a score writer and studio musician, embracing with ease and vigor a wide range of musical genres.
