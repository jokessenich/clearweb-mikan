San Diego, California

Mikan Zlatkovich has spent a lifetime at the piano — sharing stages with Tony Scott, Chet Baker, Clifford Jordan, Charles McPherson, and Louis Hayes. He has performed and recorded across the United States and Europe, carrying the fire of jazz wherever he goes. The Songbook, the blues, Bossa Nova — he plays it all with the same spirit: completely, and without reservation.

What I like most about him are his fire and spirit. He truly has a grasp of the essence of jazz piano.
